Lockpicking
---------------------------------------------------
The lock is a code made of numbers.
The length and amount of different numbers depends on the difficulty
For example
Novice [0-3]:
---	There are 3 Digits to find out
111	Is the input of the player

1--	He got 1 digit Right which is now shown
122	Second input of the player

122	He got every digit now right and the lock is now open
